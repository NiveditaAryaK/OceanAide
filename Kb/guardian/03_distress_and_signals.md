steps:
  - "Radio: use VHF channel 16. Say MAYDAY three times if life is in danger; PAN-PAN if urgent but not life-threatening."
  - "Then give name, position, people aboard, type of problem."
  - "Use visible signals (bright cloth, light, sound, flares) if available; repeat at intervals."
  - "Make signals early; waiting reduces chance of help."
  - "Stay with the vessel if possible — it is easier to spot than a person."
