steps:
  - "Wear your life jacket and clip in before conditions worsen."
  - "Reduce speed/sail early; secure loose gear and close hatches."
  - "Steer to meet waves at a slight angle (20–30° off the bow)."
  - "Avoid letting waves hit directly from the side."
  - "If overwhelmed, slow further and hold steady course."
